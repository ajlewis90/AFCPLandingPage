Frequently Asked Questions

What are the transaction fees for payments? +

Payment fees are handled by third-parties and are separate from our AI service pricing. So there is a small fee associated for this.

How do AI credits work? +

AI credits are used to power our intelligent agents and their interactions with your customers. Each conversation, product recommendation, and AI-powered action consumes credits based on complexity. Credits are included in your monthly plan and can be purchased additionally as needed.
