Frequently Asked Questions

What is AFCP? +

AFCP is an Agentic Commerce Platform that helps retailers like yourself build shopper agents. Using our platform, your business can launch and deploy a shopper agent that their customers can trust. The shopper agent can help their customers shop safely and seamlessly, just like a friend. These agents are seamless to integrate and provide immense value to businesses, consumers, and partners.

What does AFCP do? +

Our shopper agent can seamlessly take care of price negotiations, post-purchase shopping experience, and customer service for customers of businesses. For retailers, our Agentic platform can handle retail operations like product listing, pricing, inventory restocking, and demand forecasting. As a retailer, you no longer need multiple apps to achieve all this for your customers — just one agent you build once and run anywhere.

How are we different from Shopify, WooCommerce and others? +

We believe that if we crack a shopper's mind universally and build our shopper agents for trust and reliability, we can provide great value to ecommerce stores and their customers. There is an ocean of opportunity in solving problems that make a truly autonomous e-commerce ecosystem possible. AFCP's shopper agent builds capabilities that fit exactly how your business operates — no workarounds, no unused features, and no licence fees for capabilities you don't need.

Can AFCP integrate with our existing systems and tools? +

Yes, absolutely. AFCP makes life easy for you and does not replace your toolset. Rather, our platform provides you an easy-to-use interface that reduces manual effort by removing friction at various touchpoints. Your customers can shop effortlessly, never miss a deal, get timely updates, and great suggestions.

What are the transaction fees for payments? +

Payment fees are handled by third-parties and are separate from our AI service pricing. So there is a small fee associated for this and is part of the commission per sale.

How do AI credits work? +

AI credits are used to power our intelligent agents and their interactions with your customers. Each conversation, product recommendation, and AI-powered action consumes credits based on complexity. Credits are included in your monthly plan and can be purchased additionally as needed.
